Just listen to SoundCloud. That's it.

A Mac menubar utility for playing Soundcloud playlists. Soundbar plays your Soundcloud music without being needy all the time, hidden in your menu bar. Use shortcuts to skip a song or to save it for later. Fully customizable. Authored with Pavel MaÄek in Swift and Javascript.
